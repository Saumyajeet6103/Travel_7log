# TASK-03: Landing Page - "The Gang Intro"

## Goal
A dramatic, funny landing page that introduces the trip and the 7 members.

## Sections

### Hero Section
- Big title: "7 LOG KA MATHERAN TRIP"
- Subtitle with funny tagline (rotates between jokes)
- Countdown timer to trip date
- Quick stat cards: Total expenses so far, Spots planned, Days to go

### Member Cards ("The Squad")
- 7 cards, one per member
- Each card shows: Name, Nickname (funny), Emoji, Current balance (green = gets back, red = owes)
- Hover animation
- Example nicknames: "Treasurer Ji", "Google Maps Wala", "Baad Mein Deta Hu Wala"

### Navigation Cards to Modules
- Expense Tracker card → /expenses
- Itinerary card → /itinerary
- Trip Details card → /trip-details

### Footer
- "Made with ☕ and lack of sleep for 7 pagal log"
- Trip date display

## Comedy Elements
- Random rotating taglines on hero:
  - "Koi plan nahi, bas excitement hai 🏔️"
  - "7 dost, 1 shared Google Doc, aur 1000 WhatsApp messages"
  - "Sabka paisa Saumyajeet track karega... hopefully"
- Member nickname generator based on spending habits
- "Bhai teri baari hai dene ki" notification style

## UI Notes
- Dark mode first (adventure/night vibe)
- Mountain/forest color palette: deep greens, earthy browns, sky blues
- Gradient hero with subtle mountain silhouette
- Card animations with framer-motion
